# Skill: Proposal Generation

## Trigger
"write a proposal for [client]", "proposal for [vertical]", "pitch [business name]"

## Goal
Generate a one-page, outcome-focused proposal that closes the deal.

## Framework: Problem → Promise → Timeline → Price → Guarantee

### Structure

```markdown
# [Business Name] — Speed to Lead Proposal

## The Problem
[Specific pain point in their words — reference what was learned in discovery.
Always include a number: "You're missing X% of after-hours leads" or
"Your average response time is X hours — industry average is under 60 seconds."]

## The Promise
We install a system that responds to every lead within 60 seconds —
via text, email, and [voice if Tier 2] — 24/7, 365 days a year.
No lead goes uncontacted. No appointment goes unbooked.

## What Gets Built
- Instant lead response (SMS + email within 60 seconds of form submission)
- 7-touch follow-up sequence over 72 hours for non-responders
- Direct calendar booking (integrated with your existing calendar)
- No-show recovery (automated re-engagement for missed appointments)
- [If Tier 2: AI voice agent that answers calls and books appointments live]

## Timeline
- Live within 48 hours of signing
- First results visible within 7 days
- Full performance report at day 30

## Investment
[Tier 1: $1,500/month | Tier 2: $3,000/month]
One-time deployment: $[2,000-2,500]

## Guarantee
If your booked appointments don't increase by 30% in 30 days,
we refund the deployment fee. No questions asked.

## Next Step
Reply to this email or book a 15-min setup call: [booking link]
```

## Rules
- Always personalize the Problem section with the prospect's specific situation
- Use their own words when possible (from discovery call notes)
- Keep it to one page — if they need more detail, that's the setup call
- Save to `owners-inbox/proposals/[client-name]-proposal.md`
- Never send without the operator's review
